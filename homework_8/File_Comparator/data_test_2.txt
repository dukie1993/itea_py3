The FBI called Ted Kaczynski "The Unabomber" 
because his early mail bombs were sent to universities 
(UN) and airlines (A).

Amerigo Vespucci, for whom many historians believe America 
was named, was an Italian merchant.

The first Hershey’s chocolate bars with almonds were produced 
in 1908 because they were cheap to make. The nuts took the place 
of some of the more expensive milk chocolate, which meant Hershey’s 
could keep the price of the candy at a nickel.
